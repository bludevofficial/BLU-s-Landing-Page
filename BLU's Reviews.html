Review Breakdowns

There are four main factors in my reviews:

  • Atmosphere: Any pests? Clean dishes and utensils? Noise level? How comfortable am I in this establishment?
  • Price: Is the value of the experience worth what I'm being charged?
  • Food: How's the presentation? How's the flavor? How hard would it be for me to make this myself?
  • Service: This is the biggest thing that separates a restaurant from a home cooked meal. Do I feel like I'm having a better experience than if I were eating at home?
